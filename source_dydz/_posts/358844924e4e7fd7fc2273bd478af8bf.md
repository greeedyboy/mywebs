**茶****汲山川灵气****融天地精华****饮一盏红茗，染满身清香**——TeaConcentrating a natural spiritCollecting an essence of heaven and earthDrinking a cup of black tea and dye the aroma——**▼ 关于八马茶业 | ABOUT BAMA TEA**八马茶业是中国高端茗茶品牌，以弘扬中国茶文化，“让天下人享受茶的健康与快乐”为使命。八马作为中国茶代表，礼献茶叙外交，让世界爱上中国茶。

Bama Tea is a high-end Chinese tea brand, which has a mission to promote the culture of Chinese tea and "Moving the people from all over the world could enjoy the health and happiness bring out from drinking tea". As a representative of Chinese tea, Bama offers this tea as a gift in diplomacy and influences people from all over the world to fall in love with Chinese tea.**▼ 设计理念 | DESIGN DESCRIPTION**春山茗谷，草木葱茏，枝头鸟雀闹声喧，丛林骏马漫步闲。设计师为了体现如诗如画的原生态场景，将形态各异的骏马与枝繁叶茂的树进行巧妙布局组合，让画面整体具有层次感和空间感。若将系列包装一同展示，你将会看到广袤无垠的森林。Spring mountains and valleys, luxuriant green, noisy birds on the trees, horses walking in the jungle.In order to reflect the original ecological scene, the designer use svarious shapes of the horse with leafy trees in the image to produce space. By presenting this series of packaging, you would see the vast forest.

**▼ 产品设计 | PRODUCT DESIGN**为了满足不同层次的消费需求与选择，设计师通过不同的包装工艺和材质为“滇红”、“金骏眉”、“正山小种”、“老枞小种”四种茶叶设计了相应的包装。这不仅是对八马茶业品牌文化的传达，更是对品牌气质的完美展现！In order to meet the needs and choices of the consumer in different classes, the designer produces a packaging of four types of tea: “Dianhong”, “Jinjunmei”, “Zhengshan Seeds”, and“Laozong Seeds” through different techniques and materials.
