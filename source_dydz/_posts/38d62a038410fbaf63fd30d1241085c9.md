我路过你的生命，就像蚊子叮了一个包，不痛不痒；而你淡出我的生命后，犹如一把尖刀刺穿胸膛，成为我一生最重的伤。

I passed your life, just like a mosquito bite, superficial; but when you faded out my life,it seems that a knife pierced my chest, became the heaviest injury in my lifetime.
