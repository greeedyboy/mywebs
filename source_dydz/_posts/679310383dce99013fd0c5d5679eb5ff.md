**Cadesio Chocolate #**Cadesio 是一家瑞士公司，它有一个非常明确的社会目标：通过巧克力传递具有“希望”的信息，并推动人们去做出改变。Cadesio is a swiss company with a very specific social purpose: to spread a message of hope and push others to change things through chocolate.

“Ca”代表“可可”和“cambiamento”(意大利语，意为“可可”和“变化”)，“desio”在诗歌中是“渴望”的意思，对巧克力的渴望，也是对社会改变的渴望。“Ca” stands for cacao and for cambiamento ( Italian words for cocoa and change ), “desio” poetically means desire. The desire of chocolate, but also desire to change our society.

这款巧克力的包装设计由 Ester Bianchi 完成。九种巧克力，九种独特的口味，也是九个不同的故事。The chocolate packaging was designed by Ester Bianchi. Nine different kinds of chocolate, nine different flavors, nine different stories.

设计师受到故事的启发，策划了整个品牌的标识设计，并为每款巧克力设计了不同的包装，宛如九件珍贵的艺术品。Inspired by the story, the designer designed the logo of the entire brand and designed different packaging for each chocolate, just like nine precious works of art.

Hanami、Nadi、Mudita、Core、Mesa、Iride、Ventu、Gea、Santiago：每一个巧克力艺术品都是用不同的技术设计的，希望消费者在第一眼就能接收到巧克力所要传达的积极的信息。Hanami, Nadi, Mudita, Core, Mesa, Iride, Ventu, Gea, Santiago: each chocolate artwork is designed with a different technology in the hope that consumers will receive the positive message of chocolate at first sight.
